Dinosaur

Dinosaurs are fascinating creatures that lived on Earth millions of years ago during the Mesozoic Era. They were a diverse group of reptiles that varied greatly in size, shape, and behavior. Some were massive, like the long-necked sauropods or the carnivorous Tyrannosaurus rex, while others were smaller and more agile, like Velociraptor. Dinosaurs dominated terrestrial ecosystems for over 160 million years before a catastrophic event, likely an asteroid impact, led to their extinction around 66 million years ago.
